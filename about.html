About Me

Software Developer & Problem Solver

Background & Journey

From winning programming competitions to building apps that make a real difference, my journey in tech has been anything but ordinary. Starting with the basics in C++, I grabbed top spots in national contests like the Macedonian Programming Competition (3rd place in 2023) and regional Mathematics and Physics competitions (2nd place in 2022).

These experiences ignited my passion for problem-solving and led me into the exciting world of React and React Native development. Over the past year, I've dived deep into tools like React Query, Supabase, Context API, Styled Components, Tailwind CSS, React Router, and Next.js. I've built a booking app from the ground up for a hotel and developed over 10 applications that provided fresh solutions for existing websites and businesses.

But I'm not just about coding. With skills in marketing and sales, I bring a unique blend of technical know-how and business savvy to every project. I love aligning tech solutions with real-world goals to help ideas grow and thrive.

I'm all about collaboration and open communication. If you're looking for someone who's passionate about turning ideas into impactful realities, let's chat!
